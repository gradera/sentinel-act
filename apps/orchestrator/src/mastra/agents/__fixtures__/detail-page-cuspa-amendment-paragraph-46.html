In exercise of powers conferred under Section 11(1) of the SEBI Act, 1992, and in partial modification of the Master Circular for Stock Brokers, the following amendment is issued.

1. Paragraph 46 of the Master Circular for Stock Brokers dated 12 June 2023 is amended to read as follows: client unpaid securities may be retained by the stock broker only in a designated client unpaid securities account, and such securities may additionally be auto-pledged by the client in favour of the stock broker solely for the limited purpose of meeting the client's own funding obligations, subject to the safeguards specified by SEBI.

2. This circular shall come into force with immediate effect.
