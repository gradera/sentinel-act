In exercise of powers conferred under Section 11(1) of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992, SEBI hereby issues the following circular to all stock brokers.

1. All stock brokers shall implement enhanced due diligence measures for high-risk client accounts within 30 days of this circular.

2. The due diligence framework shall be reviewed annually by the compliance officer.

3. Stock brokers shall maintain records of due diligence checks for a minimum of five years.

3.1 Records shall be made available to SEBI on request within 7 working days.

3.2 Failure to maintain adequate records shall be treated as a violation of this circular.

a) The compliance officer shall certify quarterly compliance with this circular.

i. The certification shall be submitted through the SEBI intermediary portal.
