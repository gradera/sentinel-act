In exercise of powers conferred under Section 11(1) of the SEBI Act, 1992, the following circular is issued regarding client margin reporting requirements for all registered stock brokers and clearing members.

1. All stock brokers shall report client margin details to the relevant clearing corporation on a daily basis.

2. This circular shall come into force with immediate effect.
